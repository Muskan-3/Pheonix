ABOUT COMPANY

Phoenix Publicity

We are an Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising company in Pune, Maharashtra, established with an objective to change the outdoor advertising landscape in Maharashtra region. Phoenix Publicity is a registered member of Pune Outdoor Advertising Association (POAA).

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Shashikant N. Gaikwad, the company is growing rapidly since its inception in 2005 and has become one of the topmost Outdoor Advertising companies in Maharashtra.

Phoenix Publicity works with national and international brands delivering maximum visibility and brand impact through multiple advertising mediums.
